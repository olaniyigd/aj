About

Raheem Tunji Olawuyi (commonly called Ajuloopin) is a Nigerian politician. He is a member of the Federal House of Representatives of Nigeria representing Ekiti/Irepodun/Isin/and Oke-Ero federal constituency of Kwara State. He is the House Committee Chairman on Emergency and Disaster Preparedness. In 2018,the Ekiti/Irepodun/Isin/Oke-Ero Federal Constituency Bye-Election, contested across the four councils in Kwara, was declared in November as having been won by Raheem Olawuyi.

Mission

Hon. Raheem Tunji Olawuyi's mission is to represent the interest of the inhabitants of Ekiti, Irepodun, Isin and Oke-Ero local Government Areas of Kwara State at the Nigeria Federal House of Representative.
